BonsAI

BonsAI is a design to help with Separation Anxiety Disorder in adults. The design follows a double diamond process. After questionnaire and interview, we painted persona and senario, refined ideas. In the end, an prototype is made by 3D-printing and embedded device. This work achieved the highest score in final assessment of my MSc module.

Gaze Tracker

Gaze Tracker is my MSc project. It is a machine learning-based gaze estimation model built through mobile camera. The model includes a feature extraction face alignment model and a MLP network. By processing the image of human’s faces taken by front facing camera of mobile phone, the model is able to estimate their gaze points in mobile phone screen.
